How It Works

From trap to alert

Automated Pest Intelligence

JAYHUN TRAP uses species-specific pheromone lures to attract target pests into the trap chamber. A built-in high-resolution camera captures images of trapped insects, which are processed by on-device AI models for species identification and counting. Results are transmitted via 4G to the cloud dashboard in real time. Farmers receive automated alerts when pest populations exceed economic thresholds, enabling timely and targeted pesticide application — reducing chemical usage by up to 60%.
