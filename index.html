The Challenge

Agriculture faces critical threats

Modern farming in Central Asia confronts unprecedented challenges that demand intelligent solutions.

Climate Change

Lack of digitized meteorological data makes it impossible to track rapidly changing weather patterns.

Soil Salinity

The inability to continuously monitor soil leads to degradation and reduced productivity.

Diseases & Pests

Delayed response to outbreaks results in devastating crop losses and increased pesticide reliance.

Declining Yields

Mismanagement of resources causes significant damage to crop productivity.

Export Barriers

Creating pesticide-free products that meet international quarantine standards is complex.
